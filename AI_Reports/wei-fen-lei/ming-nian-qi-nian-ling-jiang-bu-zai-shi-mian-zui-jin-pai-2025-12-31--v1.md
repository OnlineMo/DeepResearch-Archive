## 背景
“明年起年龄将不再是免罪金牌”这一主题，反映了中国法律体系在未成年人违法犯罪问题上日益严谨和完善的趋势。长期以来，中国刑法对未成年人刑事责任年龄的规定相对较高，对于不满14周岁的未成年人原则上不负刑事责任，而对14至16周岁的未成年人也仅限于八类严重犯罪 [npc](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGlKi5kL07WwjznoYtJjXDS9nLdCssmQe0AicWlhGE39sg0HnwQp5I1PGW81IvFcxIOyaJvdxFPTBlr_zgAxlN_e_nuuwBOVw3iCn9xCmoBwHGOKliXG6LImn4fODDSiXHR4vOXBg0QbwPqzfi5XljAjvJXZ-2pg4Zcs2M=) [xinhuanet](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGhfMjADwBEMIjQBkXm0Z9C3IORoHMBi5-uUDxkiG_v2osLdLnHns2eKHA7KCXvEoXwbuBpKoiRYsurUI5m-dgzHIfZ-HI6m9YIX48viUfrxrkZVVV4Svl3gXv-T16uMLZ6RtoUjdK5GxnclvsLLOVorLBur9AbghQ=)。这在一定程度上被解读为“法不责幼”的传统观念，导致在少数低龄未成年人实施恶性犯罪时，社会公众普遍感到法律惩戒的缺位，引发了广泛的社会关注和对法律调整的强烈呼吁 [npc](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGgrRmO20nLI5htZM_6a6bbndyzxRDzIhbssixZKrZ7GBxckGcJmhFzECH9VouRejlqD2JrnDz7w9Zymo-cXFoED3a-8gZh0_wNKN-QF2-TKqw1MYeZFqXTIzMexUhvQVs8kAkszEK0QkBTnyw1drKiesWQzGICJB8e5FM=) [gdzjjc](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHaQVakwOSl2G3QKlhhPWAqtflTNuPSZDS2LKLvTinv0Syoyj1zS5r11pKhEXOfEyvV5T5jDXTpJxoRRQiQqYDMnQdoLHmX8wCbanbeiCN7hyRaN0KEVlBNFJX3wv-QBkHNzesGVU0yn_G0oyHw_SfJTkkYmTqBUgqnhSxtgtVZzqgsByngMjQLZBlYqs1CqafRjg==)。
