## 背景
“脚踏实地” (jiǎo tà shí dì) 是一个常用的汉语成语，字面意思是脚踩在坚实的土地上。它比喻做事认真、踏实、不虚浮，一步一个脚印地前进，常带有褒义，形容稳健和务实 [zdic](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGJPQ-xyEmcJ7U-qC_oGwKI5ST2cw5SRoF1pAu_6HaN-Nx-WdJ4TWybwVsc1ywucv_WZVpYMuUVveTuqNSpv3OggUDTL1a5C5cPz5AzL3EbQOCeuCWbw6TB-sa8wVc4fFIGtbxmsEJHEMdRHTwr7VbiT8lE2NfSj1E=)。其近义词为“足履实地”，反义词为“好高骛远” [zdic](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGJPQ-xyEmcJ7U-qC_oGwKI5ST2cw5SRoF1pAu_6HaN-Nx-WdJ4TWybwVsc1ywucv_WZVpYMuUVveTuqNSpv3OggUDTL1a5C5cPz5AzL3EbQOCeuCWbw6TB-sa8wVc4fFIGtbxmsEJHEMdRHTwr7VbiT8lE2NfSj1E=)。

这个成语的典故出自北宋时期。据宋代邵雍的《邵雍闻见前录》或《邵氏闻见录》记载，他曾评价司马光（字君实）为“君实脚踏实地人也”，意指司马光为人处事非常踏实 [zdic](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGJPQ-xyEmcJ7U-qC_oGwKI5ST2cw5SRoF1pAu_6HaN-Nx-WdJ4TWybwVsc1ywucv_WZVpYMuUVveTuqNSpv3OggUDTL1a5C5cPz5AzL3EbQOCeuCWbw6TB-sa8wVc4fFIGtbxmsEJHEMdRHTwr7VbiT8lE2NfSj1E=)。这一典故与司马光主编《资治通鉴》的经历紧密相关。司马光在编撰这部编年体通史时，展现了极其认真勤恳的治学态度，每日从凌晨工作至深夜，甚至特制“警枕”以防睡过头。他对书稿要求严格，广泛收集资料，反复修改润色，力求精简准确，例如将唐代部分的稿子从六百多卷最终定稿为八十卷。这种一丝不苟、求真务实的治学精神，正是“脚踏实地”的生动体现 [gwx5](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQF7_z3tRZZs0--FrjXJsG938kL95UNnkVE-kDJ_kImH-sx_M1PXrLH9jzpkdf_ZoJWolD0zjGpWPMxz518XEnzaHxdPMA_pEBYZtT-nkdxAdj46MXA07ClbpNvHZ1lOmtiK-bqX), [hanyuguoxue](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFUnOMJx1mqyFBYHZThJrLgFtWut1q34MOOB2J2OvdAQbrNdi3JwqZjvguzclXLhOXwcss_R1H_XqpFVEmb1ob2VyUxurLLGmaC_h_Qg5lUIJJaRbmLing9iew7E16YU4AyGDFzYGMzZ1ph)。
