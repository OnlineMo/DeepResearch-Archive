## 背景

## 深度分析

### 现象特征与演变
“撞人族”现象的显著特征是其行为的蓄意性与目标选择性。加害者常在东京池袋、涩谷、大阪心斋桥、道顿堀等人员高度密集的区域，蓄意肘击或恶意冲撞外国游客、女性、儿童、老人等被视为弱势的群体，得手后迅速逃离现场 [ycwb](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHt1vlrr9s1pqjlfjQXvoIlwm2oCRDr72xxhGq61Vh6J0qgVRY0u85ZY3Vi6li84zrETZUqzFWXDrQD2ZyLtsl4GZytHGxlzvUdN4ciAfKwpVIsfCSgYRgoW78HzKmXT84sbAxG7-r4FAd7sLnM79k=) [mirrormedia](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQG-k9NzQzsSwbTQfF7mDIugf9eZvXLYtsI5a-W-ZGDL1UvVEAD9YqY6ykjz4susBBARL29Kzimv8Tn8aShgyZ4JxjUUbQadZrOJBINE-sxtKYh7hwSWyMworMnnQcxN7UtyguVycixKk2Xq)。虽然最初多指中年男性（ぶつかり男），但近年来也出现了女性（撞人女、ぶつかりおばさん）和高龄者（撞人老人）的加害者，显示出该现象的普遍性和复杂性 [kinliu](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQExSz7p-rPz5Qf5R7kzU9T5B-tvIDXyRCXoCYM-y1T0ohT1qWJNsOnfKhqhb5Ay6kfnFKq200i26ILtL170X4cxZbCkFx7kyYVLY99GM6DuYLgiEVU1Vmc47qxq0p5_69QCqSq-vIbCrLcK-z0l8DbEDjvMQeqjt_6go5SZVgPeZpoFVBKBpzEflmZ05gpulPYwTFFEcgTEgn6QSAvO-_oJJ688wFpqm2eroam9BpbN3VbUfLQQhREr-6h2J4wwB85QX57EkpiaNTXBr0f7cVzp9aqMRFU8zCfXPqQDUHaLDZn417tMzq1D82POmT0zPM-MNtY5HkkQw_q-sq1XWRZj0QzGo3_ClylCdwMPchz5-YSzLvHD-AnmZG3Q) [wikipedia](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQEtLO6G9cTCDVLgFzKgfxU-7_lxa5VgqOci0LcWv0RxTc7G_emG3GovsVKf3-GXM6NClh2kMJBl-mQ2rRT_C86f_lT-gx1p8JLAuVajk6AuuWTaFJ_rHCUDx3itdaaxmxN1tnxiah1CcG7XihRVPNIHvY9TpA==)。

### 成因剖析
专家和旅日达人对“撞人族”现象的成因进行了多方面分析，主要包括以下几点：
1.  **社会压力宣泄（迁怒行为）**：许多故意撞人者可能处于社会底层或承受巨大工作压力，他们通过冲撞比自己弱小的人来宣泄不满，获得一种虚假的“强者权力感” [mirrormedia](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQG-k9NzQzsSwbTQfF7mDIugf9eZvXLYtsI5a-W-ZGDL1UvVEAD9YqY6ykjz4susBBARL29Kzimv8Tn8aShgyZ4JxjUUbQadZrOJBINE-sxtKYh7hwSWyMworMnnQcxN7UtyguVycixKk2Xq) [kinliu](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQExSz7p-rPz5Qf5R7kzU9T5B-tvIDXyRCXoCYM-y1T0ohT1qWJNsOnfKhqhb5Ay6kfnFKq200i26ILtL170X4cxZbCkFx7kyYVLY99GM6DuYLgiEVU1Vmc47qxq0p5_69QCqSq-vIbCrLcK-z0l8DbEDjvMQeqjt_6go5SZVgPeZpoFVBKBpzEflmZ05gpulPYwTFFEcgTEgn6QSAvO-_oJJ688wFpqm2eroam9BpbN3VbUfLQQhREr-6h2J4wwB85QX57EkpiaNTXBr0f7cVzp9aqMRFU8zCfXPqQDUHaLDZn417tMzq1D82POmT0zPM-MNtY5HkkQw_q-sq1XWRZj0QzGo3_ClylCdwMPchz5-YSzLvHD-AnmZG3Q)。
2.  **扭曲的正义感**：部分加害者抱持着扭曲的道德观，认为自己在“教训”那些不守规矩的人，例如“手机低头族”或未按规定路线行走的人 [163](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFrnsW2nve3So3JHdj1Zsj3sGYMs0UmpGcZDHjrh86gMtvsjPD-8rGpmwaLQoE1X8GqqTvgnEB122B6W6Xpv4VPCNRZpyKZKL7wpYGtpYvUebBHMLthJBNfoSmzQsMJ3rM2kc1BOPtfPgOseGOBZkI=) [cava](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQEbbSeALXoki8zINXi_T4RhT_tZFAJWiF4mv1So5U1RYFy8hZ_eK91J-JwPRAgHAvaaRcHxUA73ZykW5gv4P_ToLi6KDXGuYI-ub1iov4TSik0EL2vAlu1Lbh_A_7redmdir0OaPPPmu98=)。
