## 背景
近年来，直播电商作为一种新兴的商业模式在中国迅速崛起，食品类商品成为其中重要的销售品类。然而，伴随行业“野蛮生长”，直播电商食品销售领域也暴露出诸多突出问题，严重损害了消费者权益和市场秩序 [news](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFjycup5BuFODhUANqTsgK33NrwVA49EJWtQHgZu5IS4ZoZy1vcYxsVGDwKDexCtMCUM0X8yAk-UZ-L6y3-_Hze8dNUtHw_foJHPK0TAwlCsg78M1z4s9Crppzl-OIGG8rZF03Xq9CJyMcep1DYr9Va5EgMD-MY_IPExbeb7ZkXpdN3kz-H) [eeo](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQEJZoAb9ZNHKLOVbhjRGVRlHTif1eLnYcsa1vvXkV2aYTB7DAjtJUkuCv4CQPsNULQhl5KuNffJSrY1jJZhf3PvPhTJ1FHuSffoYbAp0xPq7OTlMxeDrjiuVoDDqQIMQ3iHmF8EmM_T)。

*   **虚假宣传与误导消费：** 主播为吸引流量和销量，常对食品的产地、成分、功能进行夸大甚至虚假宣传，尤其对老年消费者造成误导 [news](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFjycup5BuFODhUANqTsgK33NrwVA49EJWtQHgZu5IS4ZoZy1vcYxsVGDwKDexCtMCUM0X8yAk-UZ-L6y3-_Hze8dNUtHw_foJHPK0TAwlCsg78M1z4s9Crppzl-OIGG8rZF03Xq9CJyMcep1DYr9Va5EgMD-MY_IPExbeb7ZkXpdN3kz-H) [cctv](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGfyQp6d6P10XN807GXNtA3WKDL3NoMoe0tmhuTxbf3TGWQHLSKVTX8aFO2N67-8zHlg7n85epyyS9q7UpZYaXf5xaDJGw6RfePUFImiHUSoLA0Ijl-K0Ovf6verBp6N523mphOX4esn0ZLCH10APrb4C5ul9OiCFCWu97fXbXE)。
*   **假冒伪劣与食品安全隐患：** 销售过期食品、腐败变质食品、无标签预包装食品，甚至使用非食品原料或添加有毒有害物质的食品，严重威胁消费者健康 [10jqka](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQF6Oxde_PuAYF36Ue_KI4shRJZ-mTKwoph7q1vkX6xcRcGqHelWXOKcqwEE4HM7v3tKmRICl1k0EuXk7-XVtmfH3rqHG7EqCz5NZ3QcNN0Snm2povOClI92JC3uuaLZbfFU4ypqAcpiOopBjv8DZY4=) [stdaily](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFjUpa1KSgdUmR3RujDUYAxcCoAqGYzMSgprv_TBsNKjjdPKlMrmw4nj1nk640HjlQWEciOPDu-J_IgRk_2G6MltEh3-D0DmZFAvah-hdOzvVOIAF__PF1ZkWgdYyPDwt-8jMqlCj3ksRKIE7C-YXsaDaCrdG7kQp6bHg==)。
*   **责任主体模糊：** 平台、直播间运营者、主播及MCN机构之间责任界定不清，导致出现问题时相互推诿，消费者维权困难，举证成本高 [china](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQGRoFhvrJavq0O-M-T_90uf4Ao2F0dm9peQ1r4KN0jWoo7jdDZ-orY2YPgE_kBQaa5MdZmD8XTaG9DVUc59mHvGLpH2WmkcGGr9icMtUiBmt0DsRfwQ1di7S3_jjsCKfMdetBoBGoQWo3Pp3Np92o0TyUa4JA==) [stcn](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHqz8nOXLo_8m-gboK1kJyB1OojrQjxBr3hcEB-GRAM8Ha3ta2wYWmldjveZDhm4YJ50QLCyUOQlyuHwtMOGKRV1OxBxDnvGUEfZxM0BTm9q1oHc8niB5FGrz1uGhR-Xn7MRSNLMSUe0TYTBQ==)。
*   **监管措施滞后：** 现有食品安全法规对直播电商这一新业态的特殊性缺乏针对性，难以实现全链条有效监管，平台存在“只做流量、不做监管”的现象 [news](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFjycup5BuFODhUANqTsgK33NrwVA49EJWtQHgZu5IS4ZoZy1vcYxsVGDwKDexCtMCUM0X8yAk-UZ-L6y3-_Hze8dNUtHw_foJHPK0TAwlCsg78M1z4s9Crppzl-OIGG8rZF03Xq9CJyMcep1DYr9Va5EgMD-MY_IPExbeb7ZkXpdN3kz-H) [stdaily](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFjUpa1KSgdUmR3RujDUYAxcCoAqGYzMSgprv_TBsNKjjdPKlMrmw4nj1nk640HjlQWEciOPDu-J_IgRk_2G6MltEh3-D0DmZFAvah-hdOzvVOIAF__PF1ZkWgdYyPDwt-8jMqlCj3ksRKIE7C-YXsaDaCrdG7kQp6bHg==)。
