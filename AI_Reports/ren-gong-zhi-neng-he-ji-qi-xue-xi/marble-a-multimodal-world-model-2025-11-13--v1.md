## 背景
在人工智能领域，理解和模拟物理世界的能力，即“空间智能”，一直是研究的重点。由人工智能先驱李飞飞共同创立的 World Labs 公司，开发了 Marble 多模态世界模型，旨在推动这一前沿领域的发展 [worldlabs](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHjOswOa6PuILS7-5bnxynitJFPxBG09o9TxExBOJqSKGD_CMHZ52cZU-hdxPnj_orX1PQR_O48up1BUgw4xcDeAontRUYgTzSaPEwXDcWhIFQmY34BCugycKslq2dtIQxeJmZFa_p__KupoQ==) [fastcompany](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFouogqMribB1aRdo1T7Tr0bqauxXaJqPd4u746NrWf1ReN9Amgq2eW4jZzSeuXwZPer8SzmqpccK2dSU8mpWwqCm_sLQuH-YTJ5uhc-2i-ZdOxqWqoFwa5Oi8JP6R5IOIJ7DYUSOm7DtTA3BLgwZUUJnOcmv1WrZhFhcVuBrV3mgMqANgq4ushnoft) [observer](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFL1YMhKs0vJUOfrAMSmbYvsOGk88l3zVz1L2qTv3lDv5VyeFDYibzV46mXl65VNRm4aYwGfas1DrZ4i1FQ0rCg4kDdAVe6zdiOsaZjGHcNpgFcGCLvvIsZTowTN5FaBh2e1Jxmt4EJF7yIdWXiOrBryL_JLeoh6KgKqHAXyAHhnWS-cAw=)。该模型是一个生成式AI系统，专注于创建、重建和模拟3D世界，并于2025年11月全面上市 [worldlabs](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQHjOswOa6PuILS7-5bnxynitJFPxBG09o9TxExBOJqSKGD_CMHZ52cZU-hdxPnj_orX1PQR_O48up1BUgw4xcDeAontRUYgTzSaPEwXDcWhIFQmY34BCugycKslq2dtIQxeJmZFa_p__KupoQ==) [fastcompany](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFouogqMribB1aRdo1T7Tr0bqauxXaJqPd4u746NrWf1ReN9Amgq2eW4jZzSeuXwZPer8SzmqpccK2dSU8mpWwqCm_sLQuH-YTJ5uhc-2i-ZdOxqWqoFwa5Oi8JP6R5IOIJ7DYUSOm7DtTA3BLgwZUUJnOcmv1WrZhFhcVuBrV3mgMqANgq4ushnoft) [observer](https://vertexaisearch.cloud.google.com/grounding-api-redirect/AUZIYQFL1YMhKs0vJUOfrAMSmbYvsOGk88l3zVz1L2qTv3lDv5VyeFDYibzV46mXl65VNRm4aYwGfas1DrZ4i1FQ0rCg4kDdAVe6zdiOsaZjGHcNpgFcGCLvvIsZTowTN5FaBh2e1Jxmt4EJF7yIdWXiOrBryL_JLeoh6KgKqHAXyAHhnWS-cAw=)。

## 深度分析

### Marble 的核心架构能力

World Labs 的 Marble 模型代表了3D环境生成AI的重大进步，其核心能力体现在以下几个方面：
